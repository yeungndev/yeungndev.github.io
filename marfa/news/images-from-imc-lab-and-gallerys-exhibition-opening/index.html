Images from IMC Lab + Gallery’s Exhibition Opening

On October 12, The IMC Lab + Gallery held an opening reception for “Climate Art: New Ways of Seeing Data.” The exhibition is curated by Isabel Walcott Draves, and features the work of established software artists Ursula Endlicher, Ben Fry, Aaron Koblin, Nathalie Miebach, Camille Seaman, and Karolina Sobecka. These artists seek to measure the immeasurable, using data to create visual representations of the impact of climate change on the Earth and its inhabitants.

For additional information about MD/NY and the IMC Lab + Gallery, please visit their profile page.
